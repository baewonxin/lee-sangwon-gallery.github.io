Profile

SANGWON (상원) is an South Korean trainee under GRID Entertainment , set to debut as the center of ALD1 (Alpha Drive One) the Center of the group and winning in first place on the survival show BOYS || PLANET

Known for his incredible talent and powerful stage presence, he has become one of the most recognized trainees of any survival show. Before joining BOYS || PLANET he was a member of bighit trainee project TRAINEE A with fellow ALD1 member LEO.
